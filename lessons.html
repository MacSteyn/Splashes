Which Lesson is Best?

Private Swim Lessons

Our private swim lessons provide one-on-one instruction for students ages three months through adult. Private swim lessons are the best option for accelerated learning and skill development.

Semi-Private Swim Lessons

Semi-private swim lessons offer students the opportunity to interact with another swimmer of similar age and ability while still receiving personal attention from instructors.

Parent & Me Lessons

Parent and Me swimming lessons give parents the opportunity to be hands-on with their child while instructors guide the learning process.

Stroke Clinic

Stroke Clinics help students advance swimming techniques in a group setting, focusing on proper technique and preparation for swim teams.

Adult Swim Lessons

Adult swim lessons are available for swimmers ages 13 and older, designed for beginners and advanced swimmers looking to improve technique and confidence.
