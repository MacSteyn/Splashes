Which Lesson is Best?

Our Lesson Types

Private Swim Lessons

Our private swim lessons provide one-on-one instruction for students ages three months through adult. Private lessons offer accelerated learning with minimal downtime and the highest rate of progress.

Learn more here!
Semi-Private Swim Lessons

Semi-private lessons allow students ages 2.5 to 13 to learn with another swimmer of similar age and ability while still receiving personal attention in a cost-effective format.

Learn more here!
Parent & Me Lessons

Parent & Me lessons provide a fun and relaxed environment for parents and children ages 3 months through 3 years, with instructors guiding the learning process.

Learn more here!
Stroke Clinic

Stroke Clinics focus on refining swimming technique in a group setting. These classes are designed for swimmers ages 5 and older who are level 8 or higher.

Learn more here!
Adult Swim Lessons

Adult swim lessons are available for swimmers ages 13 and older, from beginners to advanced. Lessons are tailored to individual goals and comfort levels.

Learn more here!
